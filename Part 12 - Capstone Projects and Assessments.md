# Part 12: Capstone Projects & Assessments

This is your final assessment to prove you have mastered the Senior Vibe Coder workflow. You will simulate a real-world enterprise task from start to finish.

## 1. The Assignment

**Manager Request:**
*"We are a fintech startup. We need an internal dashboard that allows our support agents to view a customer's transaction history, freeze their account if suspicious activity is detected, and refund transactions. It needs to be secure and auditable."*

## 2. Your Deliverables

To pass this mentorship, you must not just produce the code. You must produce the entire enterprise trail.

### Phase A: Requirements
1. Create `BusinessRequirements.md`.
2. Create `EdgeCases.md` (Focus heavily on the "freeze account" and "refund" edge cases).

### Phase B: Architecture
3. Create `Architecture.md` defining a clean architecture.
4. Create `SecurityRules.md` defining the audit logging requirements.

### Phase C: Breakdown
5. Create `TaskBreakdown.md` listing the sequence of tasks you will feed the AI.

### Phase D: Implementation Simulation
6. Write the exact **Master Prompt** you would use to initialize the AI.
7. Write the exact **Task 1 Prompt** to begin implementation.

## 3. Final Evaluation Criteria

As your Staff Engineer Mentor, I will evaluate your Capstone on:
* **Completeness:** Did you skip planning and jump to code? (Automatic Failure).
* **Security:** Did you define audit logs for the refund action?
* **Context Engineering:** Are your prompts small and scoped, or are you trying to build the whole dashboard in one go?
* **Maintainability:** Is your architecture clearly documented so the next AI agent can understand it?

## 4. Conclusion

You are no longer a Junior Developer trying to figure out syntax. 

You are an **AI Software Architect**. You manage AI agents the way a Tech Lead manages a team of developers. You enforce rules, you define architecture, you review outputs, and you build scalable, enterprise-grade systems.
